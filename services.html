Specialized Rehabilitation Services

Physiotherapy Center in Head Injury

Head injury rehabilitation requires highly specialized and structured physiotherapy care. At Aastha RIBS, we provide comprehensive rehabilitation programs for patients recovering from traumatic brain injuries, road accidents, falls and other neurological trauma. Our therapy plans focus on improving balance, coordination, muscle strength, cognitive function and daily living skills. Each patient receives an individualized treatment protocol designed after detailed assessment by our expert therapists. The goal is to restore maximum independence, functional mobility and quality of life through advanced therapeutic techniques and continuous monitoring.

Physiotherapy Hospital Services

Aastha RIBS functions as a dedicated physiotherapy hospital offering both inpatient and outpatient rehabilitation services. Unlike ordinary physiotherapy clinics, our institute provides complete medical support, modern equipment and multidisciplinary treatment under one roof. Patients suffering from orthopedic, neurological or post-surgical conditions receive scientifically planned therapy sessions supervised by trained professionals. Our hospital environment allows continuous care, regular progress evaluation and personalized attention, ensuring faster and safer recovery for patients of all age groups.

Residential Rehabilitation Services

Many patients require long-term rehabilitation and continuous medical supervision. For such cases, Aastha RIBS offers residential rehabilitation facilities where patients can stay comfortably while undergoing intensive therapy programs. This service is especially helpful for spinal cord injury, paralysis and post-stroke patients who need daily physiotherapy, occupational therapy and nursing care. Residential rehabilitation ensures proper routine management, diet supervision, regular exercises and emotional support, creating an ideal environment for complete recovery.

Spinal Cord Injury Rehabilitation

Spinal cord injuries can cause severe physical limitations and require expert rehabilitation management. Aastha RIBS is recognized as one of the best rehabilitation centres for spinal cord injury in Patna. Our specialized programs include gait training, muscle strengthening, balance therapy, functional electrical stimulation and assistive device training. We aim to help patients regain mobility, improve bladder and bowel management and adapt to independent living. Through modern techniques and compassionate care, we guide patients toward rebuilding confidence and achieving maximum functional recovery.

Neuro Rehabilitation Center

Neurological conditions such as stroke, paralysis, Parkinson’s disease, multiple sclerosis and cerebral palsy require focused and continuous rehabilitation. Our Neuro Rehab Center provides advanced therapeutic interventions including neuro physiotherapy, occupational therapy, speech therapy and cognitive training. Each treatment plan is customized according to the patient’s condition and recovery stage. With state-of-the-art equipment and experienced therapists, we help patients relearn movements, improve coordination and gradually return to their normal daily activities.

Physiotherapy for Paralysis

Paralysis rehabilitation is a long and challenging journey that demands professional guidance and persistent effort. At Aastha RIBS, we design personalized physiotherapy programs to help paralysis patients regain muscle power, joint mobility and functional independence. Our therapists work closely with patients and families to provide gait training, strengthening exercises, posture correction and daily activity training. Regular assessments and progressive therapy methods ensure steady improvement and renewed confidence for patients suffering from hemiplegia, paraplegia or other paralysis-related conditions.
