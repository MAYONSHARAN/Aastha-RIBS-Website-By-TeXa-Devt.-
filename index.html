Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does rehabilitation take?

A: Recovery time varies depending on the condition, age and severity of the problem. After detailed assessment our specialists provide a personalized treatment schedule and realistic timeline.
Q: Do you provide residential stay facilities?

A: Yes. Aastha RIBS offers full residential rehabilitation services with medical supervision, therapy support and comfortable accommodation for long-term patients.
Q: Is physiotherapy painful?

A: Physiotherapy is designed to be safe and comfortable. Our therapists use gradual and scientific techniques to ensure minimal discomfort and maximum recovery.
Q: Do I need a doctor referral?

A: No referral is required. You can directly visit Aastha RIBS for consultation, evaluation and treatment planning.
Q: What conditions are treated here?

A: We treat stroke, paralysis, spinal cord injury, back pain, joint problems, pediatric conditions, post-surgery cases and many neurological disorders.
Q: Are services available 24×7?

A: Yes. OPD, emergency support and rehabilitation services are available round the clock for patient convenience.
