Founder

Dr. Umashankar Sinha – Founder, Aastha RIBS

Aastha RIBS was founded under the visionary leadership of Dr. Umashankar Sinha with the objective of bringing advanced rehabilitation science to the people of Bihar. With years of dedication in the field of physiotherapy and neuro rehabilitation, he established this institute to bridge the gap between modern medical treatment and effective recovery-based care. His philosophy has always been to treat every patient with compassion, patience and evidence-based therapy.

About Aastha RIBS

Aastha RIBS – Rehabilitation Institute of Brain & Spine is a dedicated multi‑disciplinary rehabilitation hospital located in Patna, Bihar. It was created with the mission of providing world‑class physiotherapy and rehabilitation services to patients suffering from neurological, orthopedic and spinal conditions. Unlike conventional physiotherapy clinics, our institute functions as a complete rehabilitation hospital equipped with modern infrastructure, advanced therapeutic equipment and trained professionals.

The institute follows a patient‑first approach where every treatment plan is carefully designed after detailed clinical assessment. We believe that rehabilitation is not only about exercises, but about restoring confidence, independence and dignity in the life of a patient. Our team works closely with patients and their families to ensure that recovery is smooth, safe and scientifically guided.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver high quality, affordable and accessible rehabilitation services using modern techniques and compassionate care. We aim to help patients regain maximum functional independence and improve their overall quality of life. Through continuous innovation and professional excellence, Aastha RIBS strives to become a center of trust and hope for individuals facing physical challenges.

Our Vision

To be recognized as the most reliable and advanced rehabilitation institute in Eastern India, setting new standards in physiotherapy and neuro rehabilitation. We envision a future where every patient, regardless of condition or background, receives the best possible opportunity to recover and return to a productive and fulfilling life.

Our Approach to Care

At Aastha RIBS we strongly believe that successful rehabilitation requires more than machines and exercises. It requires understanding the physical, emotional and social needs of a patient. Therefore our approach combines medical expertise with empathy, motivation and continuous encouragement. Each patient is guided step by step through their recovery journey with regular monitoring, progress tracking and therapy modifications as required.

From initial consultation to final recovery, our goal remains the same – to help patients stand back on their feet and return to their normal lives with renewed strength and confidence.
